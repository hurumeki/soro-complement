# 1. Game Concept

An action puzzle game where a number (the problem) is displayed at the top of the screen, and the player inputs the **complement** on a soroban (abacus), completing it so that the sum becomes "the next power of 10." Players compete to clear as many problems as possible within a 1-minute time limit, aiming for a high score.

## What is a Complement?

| Problem | Required Complement | Sum  |
|---------|-------------------|------|
| 3       | 7                 | 10   |
| 27      | 73                | 100  |
| 123     | 877               | 1000 |
